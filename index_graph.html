Innovations

Animal & Environment Entity Recognition

  • Combines YOLO object detection with improved CWA-SAM (Complex Wildlife Animal Segmentation Model) for precise segmentation of animals and irregular environmental elements
  • Optimizes SAM model through LoRA fine-tuning, achieving 99.57% segmentation success rate, a 6.87% improvement over baseline, particularly effective in complex wilderness environments

Dual-branch Animal Behavior Prediction (DABPM)

  • Fuses visual information (segmentation masks) with textual information (animal species) through multi-head attention mechanisms to predict animal-environment interaction behaviors
  • Demonstrates F1 score of 0.5923, with R@4 and mR@4 reaching 0.939 and 0.9706 respectively, excelling at handling low-frequency behaviors in long-tailed distributions

LLM-based Behavior Description Generation

  • Constructs animal-environment interaction relationships based on knowledge graphs, utilizing ERNIE model to generate natural language descriptions
  • Achieves 88.92% match rate, balancing academic accuracy with readability in generated behavior descriptions
